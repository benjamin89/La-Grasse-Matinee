Welcome to La Grasse Matinée

April 15, 2024

Are you looking for a holiday in complete peace with beautiful villages, rivers and castles and the most beautiful hiking and cycling routes? Or just passing through?
La Grasse Matinée is located in the north of the Puy-de-Dôme, a hilly area in the green heart of France, 700km from Brussels.
La Grasse Matinée is equipped with TV, fridge, gas fire, microwave, kettle, towels and sheets.
Private garden, parking and (limited) WiFi available.
No pets allowed.
Maximum of 2 people.
